# Bread Banana Pamelas

### Wet Ingredients
- 4 tablespoons butter, melted
- 1/2 cup sugar or honey
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 cup banana, mashed
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

### Dry Ingredients
- 1-3/4 cups Pamela's Baking & Pancake Mix
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup nuts (optional)

#### Directions
1. Beat together butter, sugar or honey, eggs and banana. 
2. Add remaining ingredients and mix together. 
3. Pour into a large greased loaf pan.
4. Bake in a preheated 350Â° oven for 1 hour, or until an inserted toothpick comes out clean.
